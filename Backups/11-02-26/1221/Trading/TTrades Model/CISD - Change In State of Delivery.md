## ðŸŽ¯ What is CISD?

**CISD = Change In State of Delivery**

A structure shift confirmation on a lower timeframe that validates the higher timeframe narrative. It's the **green light** that tells you the setup is playing out and it's safe to enter.

**Simple Definition:** Price breaking structure (previous high/low) and closing beyond it, confirming direction.

## ðŸ” How to Identify CISD

### **Bullish CISD:**

**What to Look For:**
1. Series of down-close candles making lows
2. Price consolidates or forms base
3. **Close above those down-close candles**
4. New protected swing established

**Step by Step:**
1. Mark the series of candles that made the low
2. Draw line at highest close of that series
3. Wait for price to close above that line
4. **That's CISD**

**Example:**
- Candles 1-3: Down closes (4810, 4805, 4800)
- Candle 4: Closes at 4815
- **CISD confirmed at 4815** âœ…

### **Bearish CISD:**

**What to Look For:**
1. Series of up-close candles making highs
2. Price consolidates or forms top
3. **Close below those up-close candles**
4. New protected swing established

## ðŸŽ¯ CISD + Protected Swing

### **Protected Swing Definition:**
A swing point (high or low) that holds and isn't broken. Used for:
- CISD confirmation reference
- Stop loss placement
- Invalidation point

### **How They Work Together:**

1. **Price makes new low**
   - Series of down-close candles
   - This is the swing being tested

2. **Price reverses**
   - Starts closing higher
   - Forms potential new swing

3. **CISD occurs**
   - Close above down-close series
   - **New protected swing established**

4. **Trade entry**
   - Stop below new protected swing
   - Trade continues in CISD direction

> *"Marking out the new low. Where is our series of down close candles? Let's see if we can get a close over that."*

## âš ï¸ CISD Red Flags

**Weak CISD (Avoid):**

- Large wick rejecting direction
- Low volume
- Immediately retraces
