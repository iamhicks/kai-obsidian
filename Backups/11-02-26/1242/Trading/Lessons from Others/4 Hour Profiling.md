# 4 Hour Profiling

A 4H profile is just a market profile on 4-hour candles instead of daily ones. It gives a sharper view of intraday bias.

## The Main 4H Profile Types

- London Reversal
- New York Continuation
- New York Reversal
- Asia Reversal

![Image 1 - What is the 4h profile?](images/4h-profile-image-1.jpg)

---

## London Reversal Profile

In a London Reversal profile, Asia consolidates or retraces. London then manipulates or reverses. New York expands.

**Timeline:**
- 2am London manipulation
- 6am New York continuation

## Summary

| Profile Type | Key Characteristic | Trading Window |
|--------------|-------------------|----------------|
| London Reversal | London reverses Asia | 2am-6am |
| NY Continuation | London sets HOD/LOD | 6am-10am |
| NY Reversal | NY sets HOD/LOD | 10am-2pm |
| Asia Reversal | Asia expands first | 6pm-6am |

**Note:** All times are approximate and based on EST/NY session timing. Adjust for your timezone (GMT = +5 hours).
