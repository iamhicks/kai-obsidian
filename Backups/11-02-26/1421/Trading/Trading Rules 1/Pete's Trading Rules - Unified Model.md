### **1. DAILY - The Filter (07:00 GMT)**
**Purpose:** Establish directional bias for the day

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Identify Daily structure (bullish/bearish)
- [ ] Mark previous day high/low
- [ ] Note any Fair Value Gaps (FVGs)
- [ ] Identify Order Blocks (OBs)
- [ ] **Only trade in direction of Daily bias**

**Rule:** If Daily is bearish, only look for shorts. Never trade against Daily bias.

### **2. 4H - The Setup (Primary Trading Timeframe)**
**Purpose:** Identify C2/C3 formations at key levels

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Price at key level (OB, FVG, previous high/low)
- [ ] C2 or C3 forming
- [ ] **Prefer C3 over C2** (higher probability)
- [ ] OHLC/OLHC profiling visible
- [ ] Room for move (not already at target)

**C3 Identification:**
- First candle after structure break = C1
- Second candle = C2 (reversal or consolidation)
- Third candle = **C3 (trade this continuation)**

**Wick Analysis:**
- Small wick = Supports expansion ‚úÖ
- Large wick = Indecision, wait ‚ö†Ô∏è

**Rule:** Never trade C2 unless it has small wick + CISD. Prefer C3 always.

## üí∞ RISK MANAGEMENT

### **Hard Rules (Never Break):**

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| **Risk per trade** | 1% of account | Maximum, can be less |
| **Target per trade** | 2% of account | Minimum, can be more |
| **Minimum R:R** | 1:2 | Non-negotiable |
| **Max daily loss** | 3% of account | Walk away after this |
| **Max trades per day** | 3-5 | Quality over quantity |
| **Max weekly loss** | 6% of account | Protect capital |

### **Position Sizing Formula:**

```
Position Size = (Account Balance √ó 0.01) √∑ (Entry - Stop Loss)

Example:
- Account: $10,000
- 1% risk = $100
- Entry: 4800
- Stop: 4780 (20 points)
- Position Size = $100 √∑ 20 points = $5 per point
```

### **Stop Loss Placement:**

**Long Trades:**
- Stop below protected swing low
- Give 10-20 points buffer
- Never widen stop once set

**Short Trades:**
- Stop above protected swing high
- Give 10-20 points buffer
- Never widen stop once set

**Rule:** Stop loss is your invalidation point. If hit, you were wrong. Accept it.

### **Target Placement:**

**Primary Target:** External Range Liquidity (ERL)
- Previous major high/low
- Fair Value Gap beyond current price
- Order Block in direction of trade
- Projection levels

**Partial Profits:**
- Take 50% off at 1:1 R:R (breakeven on runner)
- Let 50% run to 1:2 or beyond
- Trail stop to breakeven once 1:1 reached

## üß† PSYCHOLOGY FRAMEWORK

### **Daily Affirmations (Read Before Trading):**

1. **"I wait for ALL rules before entering"**
2. **"I am a risk manager first, trader second"**
3. **"One quality trade beats three forced trades"**
4. **"Missing a trade is better than taking a bad one"**
5. **"I accept losses as part of the business"**
6. **"My family is my priority; trading serves them"**

### **Emotional States to Avoid:**

**Fear of Loss:**
- **Symptom:** Hesitating on valid setups, tight stops
- **Solution:** Trust the model, follow checklist mechanically
- **Remedy:** Reduce size temporarily, build confidence

**FOMO (Fear of Missing Out):**
- **Symptom:** Chasing price, entering without full criteria
- **Solution:** Remind yourself: next setup always coming
- **Remedy:** Step away from charts, reset

**Revenge Trading:**
- **Symptom:** Trading after loss to "win it back"
- **Solution:** Stop trading for the day after 2 losses
- **Remedy:** Review rules, wait for tomorrow

**Overtrading:**
- **Symptom:** Taking every setup, even marginal ones
- **Solution:** Max 3-5 trades per day rule
- **Remedy:** Raise standards, wait for A+ setups

### **Walk Away Rules:**

- Hit daily loss limit (3%) = Done for day
- Hit 2 consecutive losses = 30 min break minimum
- Feeling emotional/frustrated = Done for day
- Family needs attention = Trading stops immediately
- After 3 winning trades = Consider stopping (protect profits)
