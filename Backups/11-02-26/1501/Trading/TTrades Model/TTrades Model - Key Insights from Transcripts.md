## ðŸ” CHANGE IN STATE OF DELIVERY (CISD)

**Definition:** Structure shift confirmation on lower timeframe

**Purpose:**
- Validates higher timeframe narrative
- Confirms entry timing
- Reduces false entries

**How It Works:**
1. Price breaks structure (previous high/low)
2. New swing point formed
3. Close above/below confirms shift

**Example (Bullish):**
- Price makes new low
- Then closes above series of down-close candles
- This is CISD confirmation âœ…

> **Quote:** "We want to see a close over that series of down close candles. That means we can be looking for continuations."

**Protected Swing:**
- Swing point that holds (not broken)
- Used for stop loss placement
- Invalidation point

> **Quote:** "Marking out the new low. Where is our series of down close candles? Let's see if we can get a close over that."

### **Protected Swing:**
- Swing point that hasn't been broken
- Confirms trend continuation
- Used for stop placement

**How to Identify:**
- Price makes new high/low
- Closes in direction of move
- Not broken by subsequent candles

### **Stop Loss Placement:**
- Below protected low (for longs)
- Above protected high (for shorts)
- Gives trade room to breathe
- Logical invalidation point
