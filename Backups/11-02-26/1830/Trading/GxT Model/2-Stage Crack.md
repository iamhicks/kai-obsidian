## ðŸŽ¯ What is 2-Stage Crack?

**2-Stage Crack = Price breaking through key levels in two distinct stages**

Instead of one clean break, price:
1. **Stage 1:** Initial break/test of level
2. **Consolidation/Retest:** Price hovers, tests, pulls back

**Purpose:** Filter out false breaks and identify genuine moves
