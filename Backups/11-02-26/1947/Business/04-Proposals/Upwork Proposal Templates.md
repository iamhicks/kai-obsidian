## Proposal Writing Tips

**Do:**
- Reference specific details from their job post
- Offer a small sample upfront (50-100 records)
- Give timeline and price
- Keep it under 150 words
- End with a question

**Don't:**
- Use generic copy-paste
- Talk about yourself too much
- Be vague about deliverables
- Overpromise on timeline

**Follow-up Message (if no response in 3 days):**

> Hi [Name], just following up on my proposal. I'm starting a similar project next week but wanted to check if you still need help with [specific task]. Happy to jump on a quick call if easier. Thanks, Pete
