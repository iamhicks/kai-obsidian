# Resources Library

## Essential Resources for Home Education

### Free Resources

**Libraries (Most Important)**
- Local public library (books, events, museum passes)
- Russian language library if available
- Interlibrary loan for specialized books
- Librarians as resources (ask for recommendations!)

**Online Free**
- **Khan Academy Kids** (app, bilingual options)
- **PBS Kids** (quality educational content)
- **Cosmic Kids Yoga** (YouTube, physical activity)
- **National Geographic Kids** (nature/science)
- **Storyline Online** (actors reading children's books)
- **Project Gutenberg** (free classic books)
- **Librivox** (free audiobooks)

### Worth Paying For

**Books (Physical)**
- **"The Read-Aloud Handbook" by Jim Trelease** (parent guide)
- **"How Children Learn" by John Holt** (education philosophy)
- **"The Brave Learner" by Julie Bogart** (homeschool practical)
- **"The Whole-Brain Child" by Dan Siegel** (brain development)
- **"Simplicity Parenting" by Kim John Payne** (declutter childhood)

**Magazines**
- Ranger Rick (nature)
- Cricket Magazine (stories)
- National Geographic Kids
- Highlights (puzzles, stories)

**Apps (Limited, High Quality)**
- **Epic!** (digital library, worth subscription)

**Subscriptions**

## Russian Language Resources

### Books (Physical)

**Essential Russian Children's Books:**
- **Корней Чуковский** — «Мойдодыр», «Тараканище», «Айболит» (classic poetry)
- **Самуил Маршак** — «Двенадцать месяцев», «Кошкин дом»
- **Эдуард Успенский** — «Чебурашка», «Простоквашино»
- **Владимир Сутеев** — «Мешок яблок», «Под грибом»
- **Николай Носов** — «Незнайка» series (for older kids)

**Bilingual/Bicultural:**
- «Колобок» (Russian gingerbread man)
- «Репка» (The Turnip - cumulative tale)

## What NOT to Buy

❌ **Worksheets/workbooks** (developmentally inappropriate, boring)
❌ **Flashcards** (rote memorization, AI does this)
❌ **Educational apps that are just games** (waste of time)
❌ **"Academic" preschool curriculum** (too early)
❌ **Toys with only one use** (limit creativity)
❌ **Expensive "educational" toys** (marketing > value)

## Russian-Specific Annual Events

**Calendar:**
- **January:** Old New Year (Рождество, if Orthodox)
- **February/March:** Maslenitsa (Масленица) — pancake festival
- **April:** Easter (Пасха)
- **Summer:** Kupala Night (Ивана Купала) — midsummer
- **November:** National Unity Day
- **Year-round:** Cultural events at Russian centers

**Participate when possible** — cultural immersion is powerful.
