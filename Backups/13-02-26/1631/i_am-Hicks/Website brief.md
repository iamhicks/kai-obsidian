## 3. PRODUCT: MIND

### What It Is
MIND is a beautiful, offline-first knowledge base for people who think deeply. It's where ideas grow, connections form, and knowledge compounds.

### Key Features
| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Rich Text Notes** | Write, format, organize your thoughts |

| **Folder Organization** | Nested folders, favorites, drag-and-drop sorting |
| **Local Storage** | Your notes live on your machine, nowhere else |
| **Markdown Support** | Import/export, full markdown compatibility |
| **Dark Mode** | Easy on the eyes, focused writing environment |

### Pricing
- **$39 one-time purchase**
- No subscriptions
- No upsells
- Free updates for life

### Target User
- Knowledge workers who value privacy
- Writers, researchers, thinkers
- People tired of Notion's complexity or Evernote's bloat
- Anyone who wants their notes to stay private

## 12. SEO REQUIREMENTS

### Search Engine Optimization

**Goal:** Rank for keywords related to offline notes, privacy-focused productivity, and alternatives to subscription-based tools.

### Target Keywords

**Primary (High Priority):**
- offline notes app
- private note taking app
- alternative to Notion
- alternative to Evernote
- buy once own forever software
- no subscription notes app
- local AI note taking
- offline knowledge base

**Secondary (Medium Priority):**
- secure note taking (without cloud)
- markdown notes app
- simple note app
- distraction free writing app
- AI writing assistant offline
- note app for writers
- note app for researchers

### Technical SEO Requirements

**Meta Tags (Every Page):**
- Unique title tag (50-60 characters)
- Meta description (150-160 characters)
- Open Graph tags for social sharing
- Canonical URLs

**URL Structure:**
- Clean, readable URLs: `/mind/` not `/product?id=123`
- Hyphens not underscores: `/help-guide/` not `/help_guide/`

**Content Structure:**
- One H1 per page
- Logical H2, H3 hierarchy
- Alt text on all images
- Internal linking between pages
- Fast loading (no heavy scripts)

**Page Speed:**
- No animations (already covered)
- Optimized images
- Minimal JavaScript
- Static HTML where possible

### Content Strategy for SEO

**Blog/Content Ideas (Post-Launch):**
- "Why I Built an Offline Note App"
- "The Problem with Cloud-Based Notes"
- "How to Organize Your Thoughts (PARA Method)"
- "Notion vs Obsidian vs MIND: A Comparison"
- "Why Subscriptions Are Killing Software"

**FAQ as SEO Content:**
Every FAQ answer should target a specific search query:
- "Is there a notes app that works offline?" → Yes, MIND...
- "What's the best alternative to Evernote?" → MIND is...
- "Can AI work without internet?" → Yes, MIND uses local AI...

### Local SEO (If Applicable)

Not applicable — digital product, no physical location.

### Measurement

**Track:**
- Organic search traffic
- Keyword rankings (target keywords)
- Click-through rate from search results

**Tools:**
